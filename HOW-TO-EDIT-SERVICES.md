# How to Edit "Other Meaningful Ceremonies" Section

The "Other Meaningful Ceremonies" section (Child Naming, Funerals, etc.) is now fully editable through your WordPress admin panel.

## Where to Find It

Go to: **Other Services > All Other Services** in your WordPress dashboard

## Adding a New Service

1. Click **"Add New"** button
2. Fill in the fields:
   - **Title**: Service name (e.g., "Child Naming Ceremonies")
   - **Content**: Full description of the service
   - **Icon Type**: Choose from dropdown:
     - Child/Family (people icon)
     - Heart (for memorials/funerals)
     - People/Community (group icon)
     - Celebration (sparkles icon)
   - **Button Text**: Call-to-action (e.g., "Celebrate Your Child")
3. Click **"Publish"**

## Editing Existing Services

1. Go to **Other Services > All Other Services**
2. Click on the service you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Click **"Update"**

## Changing Service Order

Services appear on the homepage from left to right based on their order:

1. Go to **Other Services > All Other Services**
2. Click on a service to edit it
3. Find the **"Display Order"** field in the "Service Details" box (easy to find!)
4. Set order numbers (0, 1, 2, etc.)
   - Lower numbers appear first (left)
   - Higher numbers appear last (right)
5. Click **"Update"**

## Deleting a Service

1. Go to **Other Services > All Other Services**
2. Hover over the service you want to delete
3. Click **"Trash"**

## Current Services to Add

When you first install this theme, you'll need to add these two services:

### Service 1: Child Naming Ceremonies

- **Title**: Child Naming Ceremonies
- **Icon Type**: Child/Family
- **Button Text**: Celebrate Your Child
- **Content**:
```
A non-religious, joyful celebration of new life. Honouring the child and welcoming them into the community with meaningful readings and symbolic acts.
```
- **Order**: 0 (appears first/left)

### Service 2: Funerals & Memorials

- **Title**: Funerals & Memorials
- **Icon Type**: Heart
- **Button Text**: Arrange a Tribute
- **Content**:
```
Warm, authentic, and compassionate services that honour the life lived. Creating a dignified and personal tribute that brings comfort and reflection.
```
- **Order**: 1 (appears second/right)

## Tips

- Keep descriptions concise (2-3 sentences max)
- Button text should be action-oriented
- You can add as many services as you want
- Services will display in a responsive grid
- If no services are created, default hardcoded services will display
